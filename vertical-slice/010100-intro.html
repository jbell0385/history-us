Ancient Greece & Rome

Learning Target

I can compare and contrast democratic influences from ancient Greece and Rome.

Lesson Objectives
  1. Identify and describe ancient Greek history and democratic structures
  2. Identify and describe Roman history and democratic structures.
  3. Understand the historic context of the rise of democracy during the Enlightenment.
